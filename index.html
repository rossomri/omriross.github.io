Research Objectives

My passion for pioneering research at the intersection of applied computer science and financial technologies is matched by my admiration for entrepreneurship. During my work as an Assistant Professor at DIKU, I have continuously sought to build bridges between industry and academia. This pursuit has led me to organize and co-organize a sequence of successful events, bringing leading industry figures to DIKU in the effort of providing our students with exposure to the challenges faced by leading Danish and international firms.
